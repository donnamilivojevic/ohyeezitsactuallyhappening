Directions

Public Transport

  • ​Take the subway to Ropsten
  • Change to Lidingöbanan towards Gåshaga
  • Get off at Högberga (station after Kottla)
  • Cross the tracks and take a right. Go straight down to the turnaround and the traffic lights
  • Cross crosswalk and follow the signs on which it says Högberga Gård​

By car

  • Follow the signs towards Lidingö via Norra Länken. Norra länken can be reached from Roslagstull and also from E4 and E18 from north
  • After Lidingöbron follow the signas towards GÅSHAGA
  • After about 6 km follow the signs towards HÖGBERGA GÅRD
Free parking on the area

By boat

You can take the Waxholmsbåt from Blaiseholmskajen below Grand Hôtel to Gåshaga Brygga and then take the Lidingöbanan to station HÖGBERGA. The boat ticket costs 60 SEK/person and total travel time by boat and ​tram is 45 min.
